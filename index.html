Definitions
"Current curse:" indicates curses in general
"Current curse by Author:" indicates curses only by the Author (e.g. MaxDudeIGuess - The author of Blocurses)
"Current [type of curse] curse:" indicates the general curses (e.g. Crabs - including Firestix, TQG, and Grafrustix)
"Current meta:" indicates... well... things that are not related to ADOFAI whatsoever- (I really do not know... This indication is made-up btw)
